Bluwhale AI
Bluwhale is Web3’s Intelligence Layer, a personalization protocol empowering dApps (Games, Defi, Protocols, L1/L2s, Exchanges) with insights into their user wallets using AI for profiling, targeting, and discovery of potential customers. The company uses AI to aggregate and contextualize user on-chain and off-chain information and matches enterprises with wallet holders for the highest probability of engagement like through messaging (on/off-chain). This will help projects aggressively grow their user base, while consumers can turn their profile and data into a profitable asset.
